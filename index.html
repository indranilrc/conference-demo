Call for Papers

We invite submissions of original research papers, case studies, and technical presentations on topics related to renewable energy, energy economics, carbon capture, digital transformation in energy, and sustainable energy policy. Submission deadline: June 15, 2023.

About the Conference

The International Energy Conference brings together leading researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss the latest innovations and challenges in the energy sector. Join us for three days of insightful panels, workshops, and networking opportunities focused on sustainable energy solutions and technological advancements.
